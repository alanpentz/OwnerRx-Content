How to Increase Your Valuation
The Sellability Test
Ask yourself: If you disappeared tomorrow, would the business survive 6 months?

If the answer is no, you don't have a sellable business - you have a job. And jobs aren't worth anything to buyers.

Industry Valuation Multiples (Reality)

Services: 1.5-3x EBITDA (if owner not required)
SaaS: 3-6x ARR (if growing 20%+)
E-commerce: 2-4x EBITDA
Manufacturing: 3-5x EBITDA
Retail: 1-2x EBITDA

Remember: These assume a well-run, transferable business. Most small businesses sell for much less or don't sell at all.
